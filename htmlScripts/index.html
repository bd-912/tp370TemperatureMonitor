Welcome to our Temperature Website!

In this website you will find acurate readings of temperatures and bonus humididty measurements in your home so you may better use your Air Conditioning and/or Heating Unit. By using RaspberryPi devices in addition to a Gowoops sensor we present this temperature/humidity data to allow users the benefit to precisely determine when it's the most efficent times to use your HVAC units to save money.
